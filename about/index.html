    Overview¶

    Ye is a research scholar at University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, is also a PhD student (advised by Yuanjing Feng) at Zhejiang University of Technology, working on Diffusion MRI. He will receive his PhD in Computer Science at Zhejiang University of Technology, China in December, 2018. For his PhD research, he worked on Diffusion-MRI-based neuroimage computing. He spent the last second year of his PhD study as a visiting scholar (advised by Lauren J O' Donnell and Fan Zhang) at the Laboratory of Mathematics in Imaging (LMI) and the Surgical Planning Laboratory (SPL) at Harvard Medical School (HMS) and Brigham & Women's Hospital (BWH), focusing on tractography precellation. His current research interests are diffusion imaging of the brain and modeling of structural relationships for baby analysis. He currently advised by Pew-Thian Yap.
